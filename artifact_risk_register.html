A 12-month simulated risk register maintained across HIPAA, NIST CSF, and ISO 27001 control domains for a small-business baseline. Built to mirror how a working GRC analyst tracks, scores, and closes risk over a full assessment cycle — not a one-time snapshot.

Review Cadence
Methodology: Risk score = Likelihood × Impact (1–3 scale each), producing a 1–9 tier: 1–3 Low, 4–6 Medium, 7–9 High. Each risk is reviewed at minimum quarterly; High-tier risks are reviewed monthly until remediated or formally accepted. Risks originate from control gap analysis, incident review, and vendor onboarding screening.
