About Logical Coders

Logical Coders is run by Anél Henning, a graphic designer and creative director with twenty years of brand, publication, and digital design experience — most recently through her own studio, Logika Studios.

Compliance documentation turned out to share a core problem with design work: most of it fails not because the underlying rules are wrong, but because nobody can actually follow what's written. Twenty years spent translating complicated ideas into clear, usable communication for clients across healthcare, retail, and creative industries carries over directly into writing policies, risk registers, and audit evidence that people can actually use — not just file away.

Logical Coders was started to bring that same clarity to HIPAA and NIST CSF-aligned documentation for small businesses that need it done right, without hiring a full compliance department.
