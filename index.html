Why a designer runs a compliance documentation practice

Twenty years of translating complicated ideas into clear, usable communication as a graphic designer and creative director turned out to be exactly the skill compliance documentation needs. Most policy documents fail not because the rules are wrong, but because nobody can understand them. That's the gap Logical Coders fills.
