Services

Documentation-first compliance support for small businesses working toward HIPAA, NIST CSF, or a similar framework — without needing to hire a full-time compliance department.

Policy & Procedure Writing

Policies your staff can actually follow

Written policies and procedures aligned to HIPAA and NIST CSF, in plain language rather than legal boilerplate — so the people who have to follow them actually can.

Risk & Gap Analysis

Risk registers & gap analysis

A structured risk register mapped to your chosen framework, identifying where your current controls fall short and tracking remediation over time.

Audit Preparation

Audit evidence package preparation

Evidence organized, labeled, and version-controlled so it's ready to hand to an auditor or assessor — instead of assembled the week before a deadline.

Ongoing Tracking

Compliance calendars & vendor risk tracking

Recurring compliance obligations and third-party/vendor risk tracked in one place, with clear ownership and due dates.

Remediation

Corrective action plan (CAP) tracking

Audit findings turned into a tracked remediation plan, with clear owners, target dates, and status — so findings actually get closed.

How an engagement works

Step 1

Discovery call

A short conversation about your current documentation, the framework you're working toward, and where the gaps are.

Step 2

Documentation sprint

Policies, registers, or evidence packages drafted and reviewed with you in structured rounds — not a single black-box handoff.

Step 3

Review & handoff

Final documents delivered in the format your team actually uses, with a walkthrough of how to maintain them going forward.
